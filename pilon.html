About Us
Cuban Food,
Done Right.

Pilón is a Jacksonville-based Cuban food truck dedicated to one thing — serving authentic Cuban food the way it's meant to be made. No shortcuts, no substitutions.

Every item on our menu is a traditional Cuban dish prepared fresh daily. From the Lechón Asado to the pressed Cubano, everything is made with the same ingredients and techniques that define Cuban cuisine.

We take pride in what we serve and it shows in every plate.

Gabriel Pérez
Owner, Pilón Authentic Cuban
